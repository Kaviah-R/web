About Our IT Department

Excellence in Education, Innovation, and Technology

Welcome to the Department of IT

The Department of Information Technology was established with the mission of producing highly skilled professionals in the field of computer science and information systems. We focus on practical learning, industry-oriented training, and innovative research.

Vision

The vision of the department is to cultivate a technological women community of enlightened and engaged citizens by empowering the digital revolution, transforming with industry skills and improving lives through efficiency, productivity, and innovation.

Mission

  • To develop a culture of innovation driven entrepreneurship by inculcating the professional and moral values to meet the current era.
  • To encourage students to become problem solving individual by providing an efficient teaching and learning environment through technological resources.
  • To produce successful graduates with personal and professional responsibilities and commitment to lifelong learning..
  • To prepare students for global career opportunities and higher studies.
